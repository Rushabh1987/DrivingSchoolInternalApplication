# UI Flows

## Admin Sign In

1. Open the app.
2. Enter the admin username and password.
3. The dashboard opens after successful sign in.
4. The session expires after 12 hours or when the browser tab is closed.

## Register Student

1. Click `Add Student` from the header or dashboard quick actions.
2. Fill required fields: student name, phone number (exactly 10 digits), joining date, and status.
3. Fill optional details if available: total fees, address, and date of birth.
4. Click `Save Student`.
5. If the phone number is not 10 digits, the app shows a validation error.
6. If the phone number already exists, the app shows a duplicate warning.
7. If saving succeeds, the dashboard opens and shows the student in `Recently Added`.
8. The student is also visible in the `Students` view.

## View Student Profile

1. Open the `Students` view.
2. Click `View` or `View Profile` on any student row or card.
3. The profile shows personal details, course and permit info, a payment summary bar, training days, payment history, notes, and activity log.

## Edit Student

1. Open the student profile.
2. Click `Edit`.
3. Update name, phone (10 digits), joining date, status, fees, date of birth, or address.
4. Click `Save Changes`.
5. The profile refreshes and the students list updates in the background.

## Archive Student

1. Open the student profile.
2. Click `Archive`.
3. A `Confirm Archive` button appears — click it to proceed.
4. The student is archived and removed from the active students list. Archived students remain searchable with the `All` filter.

## Add Training Day

1. Open the student profile.
2. In the Training Days section, click `Add Training Day`.
3. Fill in the date (required), time, status, and instructor name.
4. Click `Save`.
5. The training day appears in the list immediately.

## Edit Training Day

1. Open the student profile.
2. In the Training Days section, click `Edit` on the training day row or card.
3. Update the fields and click `Save`.
4. The list refreshes with the updated values.

## Delete Training Day

1. Open the student profile.
2. In the Training Days section, click `Delete` on the training day row or card.
3. A `Confirm` button appears — click it to proceed.
4. The training day is removed from the list.
